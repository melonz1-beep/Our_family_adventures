# Our Family Adventures 10.3.4

Scrapbook Studio mobile editing and layout upgrade.

## Included fixes

- Adds direct drag handles for resizing photo frames, stickers, and text objects.
- Separates frame resizing from photo zooming and positioning inside a frame.
- Replaces sticker text prompts with direct sticker selection and resizing.
- Replaces color-only themes with complete illustrated scrapbook papers, borders, and decorations.
- Moves Add, Undo, Redo, and Edit into the top mobile toolbar.
- Keeps Back to App at the far-left top position and removes the bottom toolbar.
- Numbers photos directly on the canvas and shows numbered image thumbnails in the layer list.
- Adds checkboxes, Select all, Clear, and Edit checked photos together controls.
- Adds an Add frame to every photo option, with checked-photo frame editing as an alternative.

## Cache

Service worker cache: `ofa-10-3-4`

After deployment, close every app tab, open `?v=10.3.4` in the browser, and then reopen or reinstall the home-screen app.
