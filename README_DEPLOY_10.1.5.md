# Our Family Adventures 10.1.5

## Scrapbook improvements
- Full-page scrapbook viewer now scales the complete portrait or landscape page to the available screen instead of showing only part of it.
- Frames and cutouts are combined into one Photo Shape & Frame selector.
- Shaped frames use a layered mat/border treatment rather than a marker-like outline.
- Frame shadow and glow are preserved in finalized pages, full-page viewing, PDF export, and printing.
- Added themed backgrounds: nautical, road trip, wedding, holiday, autumn, winter, watercolor, linen, floral, and chalkboard, in addition to existing themes.

## Deploy
Replace the repository files with this package, commit, and open the app with `?v=10.1.5` once.

No Firebase rules or Cloud Function deployment is required.
